About

Stephanie Kearley Müller is an artist from Louisiana, USA, living in Germany. Having acquired a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree from Louisiana Tech University in 2008, she continues to produce artwork while pursuing a Master's degree at the Pädagogische Hochschule in Karlsruhe.

As a child she was raised in a strict Protestant household but was greatly influenced by the colorful and unique Louisiana lifestyle. In 2008, she moved to China, which is where she met her Russian husband, and in 2010, she moved to Germany, learned the language and continued her education.

This mixed background inspires her artwork, which primarily consists of figurative oil paintings that express her exploration of culture, spirituality, and innocence. Full of rich colors and patterns, her paintings illustrate the collision of worlds that she experiences everyday and are her way of trying to figure out where she belongs in a world that is much more complex than she originally thought.
